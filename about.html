About Me

Prior to starting my career as a games programmer, I graduated with a degree in Physics from the University of Bath. Over the past eighteen months, I've been doing a level 7 games programming apprenticeship with Sumo Digital via the Sumo Academy.


Here, I've gained incredible experience working on several real-world commercial projects. I've worked closely with experienced industry veterans from a wide range of disciplines including artists, designers, producers and directors. I've worked with publishers and stakeholders to develop games with a shared vision.


I've developed an interest particularly in the sensory programming disciplines: Audio, Graphics, Gameplay and Physics. I've worked with graphics API's, written many shaders, implemented a multitude of game mechanics and systems, and I've worked with physics API's to implement unique physical behaviour.


Please take a look at my projects to get a better understanding of the skills I've developed!
